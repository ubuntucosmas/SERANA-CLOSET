# SERANA CLOSET - Project Strategy & Requirements ## 1. Brand Direction **Positioning:** Elegant. Custom. Effortless. **Tagline:** "Custom Wear. Timeless Confidence." **Visual Identity:** Premium, luxury feel with a focus on precision and identity. ## 2. Website Structure & Key Sections ### 🏠 Homepage * **Hero:** High-quality imagery of custom designs. CTA: "Shop Now" & "Order Custom Design". * **Categories:** Ladies Wear (Primary), Custom Designs, Ready-to-Wear, Men’s Collection. * **How It Works:** 1. Choose/Browse -> 2. Submit Measurements -> 3. Tailor & Deliver. * **Featured Products:** Clean grid layout. * **Final CTA:** "Have a design in mind? Let’s create it for you." -> Contact/Custom Order. ### 🛍️ Core Pages * **Shop Page:** Filterable by category, price, and size. * **Custom Order Page (The "Goldmine"):** Simple form for inspiration uploads, fabric selection, and measurements. * **Contact Page:** Integrated WhatsApp button (critical), Phone, and Social links. * **About Page:** Short, punchy brand story. ## 3. UI/UX Strategy * **Style:** Minimalist, high whitespace, large imagery. * **Colors:** Black (#000), Soft Gold (#C9A96E), White (#FFF). * **Typography:** Elegant Serif for headings, clean Sans-serif for body. * **Key Feature:** "Auto WhatsApp Order" to increase conversions. * **Performance:** Mobile-first, lazy loading, optimized assets. ## 4. Tech Stack (Laravel + Vue.js) * **Backend:** Laravel APIs for Products, Orders, and Custom Requests. * **Frontend:** Vue.js components (ProductCard, CategoryFilter, CustomOrderForm, WhatsAppButton).
